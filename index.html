AJITHA RESIDENCE

Ajitha Residence, located in Alappuzha and commissioned by Mrs. Ajitha, is a modern home distinguished by its contemporary exterior and meticulous brick cladding detailing. The design seamlessly blends traditional brickwork with sleek, modern architectural elements, creating a visually striking facade. Spanning a built-up area of 1900 sqft, the residence embodies a sophisticated aesthetic while harmonizing with the surrounding landscape of Alappuzha, known for its serene backwaters and lush greenery. The project, initiated in 2023, is currently ongoing.

Year: 2024
Area: 1820 sqft

ASIF VILLA

Asif Villa, located in Nilambur, a 2700 square foot modern architectural project, exemplifies minimalism and elegance in residential design. The villa boasts clean lines, open spaces, and a harmonious blend of functionality and aesthetics. The interiors are meticulously curated to emphasize simplicity, utilizing a neutral color palette, natural materials, and ample natural light to create a serene and sophisticated living environment. Each element, from the thoughtfully designed layout to the carefully selected furnishings, contributes to a cohesive and tranquil atmosphere, making Asif Villa a model of modern elegance and understated luxury

SHAJI UMMER RESIDENCE

Shaji Ummer Residence, located in the serene town of Kochi, is a captivating two-story home covering 3170 square feet, thoughtfully crafted for Mr. Shaji Ummer and his family. This exquisite residence features four generous bedrooms, each with its own attached bathroom, ensuring the highest level of privacy and comfort for all occupants. The design philosophy harmoniously integrates modern aesthetics with functional efficiency, resulting in a cozy yet highly practical living space that perfectly meets the needs of its residents. A standout feature is the floating gazebo, which is designed to delight all who see it. The ample glass areas throughout the house ensure abundant natural light, enhancing the overall ambiance and warmth of the home.

BIJU SAM RESIDENCE

Biju Sam Residence in Trivandrum is a 2000 square foot, 4-bedroom home that epitomizes minimalist design both inside and out. The exterior features clean, unadorned lines and a simple yet striking aesthetic that blends seamlessly with its surroundings. Inside, the residence continues this theme of minimalism, with a focus on functionality and uncluttered spaces. The interiors are designed with a neutral color scheme, incorporating natural materials and strategic lighting to enhance the sense of openness and tranquility. Each room is thoughtfully arranged to maximize space and comfort, creating a harmonious and inviting living environment. The Biju Sam Residence stands as a testament to the beauty of minimalism in modern residential architecture.
